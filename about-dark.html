My Team

If you take any education system, there are many stages like primary grade, intermediate grade, higher grade etc., there are also many stages in the spiritual field. Based on the Bible, the followers of Jesus were divided into many divisions such as public, believers, disciples and apostles. Within this each division had separate sub-divisions also based on their commitments.

I adopt the same pattern in my spiritual ministry. Since the day the Lord anointed me and set me apart, there were many people who received good things from the Lord and left with grateful hearts and ungrateful hearts. Dinesh, Kala Kamal and Dayabai who were connected in disciple level - primary grade were also with me in the first stage.

There are certain criteria to release them from the primary grade of discipleship to go independently into their own vision. As such Dinesh, Kala Kamal and Dayabai were released in early September 2022 to proceed on their own terms.

At the same time, those who personally agree to the upgradation process from disciple level - primary grade will be assigned to intermediate grade.The difference in intermediate grade is that there is no freedom to do anything on your own without permission. Only a person who has completely surrendered himself to God, starting with his decisions, wishes and desires, can continue in the intermediate grade. Whenever these standards are not met then they are released from this level for self-determination.

At present those who stand at each level -

Those who connect at the public level are a crowd that comes and goes.
The Lord has graciously opened a platform to minister to a few people who are still connected at the believers level.
In primary grade we now have Binu Samu, Vinduja, Vichithra, Anishma, and Aldrin.
Now only Kala Kamal is available in intermediate grade.

If the Lord is adding people to His kingdom daily, then the Lord will give the opportunity to join only those who are appointed by God for the work that God wants to do through me. Therefore, there is a possibility that anyone who is at each level can leave this group at any time, and there is also a possibility that new people can be brought in without looking at their qualifications.
